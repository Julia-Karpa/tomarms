Blog & Podcasts


As an American living in London, Tom Arms writes a weekly blog entitled “Observations of an Expat.” The blog consists of an in depth analysis of a major event or a written insight into an important topic. Following the major article there is a review of the world’s major world events. The blog is free, although subscribers have the option to pay a voluntary contribution.
