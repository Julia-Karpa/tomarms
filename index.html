About Tom

London-based Tom Arms is a respected foreign affairs author, journalist, blogger, lecturer, and broadcaster with more than 50 years’ experience of reporting and analysing international relations. He is currently the foreign editor of Liberal Democrat Voice and writes a regular blog—“Observations of an Expat.” Past positions include: foreign correspondent, diplomatic correspondent, foreign editor, editor, and founding editor and CEO of an international news service. Tom’s books include “The Falklands Crisis,” “The Encyclopaedia of the Cold War,” and “America Made in Britain.” He regularly lectures on world affairs and is a commentator on radio and television programmes around the world.
