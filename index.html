Foreign Affairs Expert

London-based Tom Arms is a respected and experienced foreign affairs journalist, author, broadcaster and lecturer. He is currently foreign editor of Liberal Democrat Voice and writes a weekly blog entitled “Observations of an Expat.” Previous positions include: foreign correspondent, diplomatic correspondent, foreign editor, editor and founding editor and CEO of Future Events News Service—an international news agency used by thousands of journalists around the world to plan their news coverage and by governments and multinationals to plan their events. Tom is also the author of “The Falklands Crisis,” “The Encyclopedia of the Cold War” and “America Made in Britain.”
