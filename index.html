London-based Tom Arms is a respected foreign affairs author, journalist, blogger, lecturer and broadcaster with more than 50 years experience of reporting and analysing international relations. He is currently foreign editor of Liberal Democrat Voice and writes a regular blog—“Observations of an Expat.” Past positions include: foreign correspondent, diplomatic correspondent, foreign editor, editor and founding editor and CEO of an international news service. Tom’s books include: “The Falklands Crisis,” “The Encyclopaedia of the Cold War,” and “America Made in Britain.” He regularly lectures on world affairs and is a commentator on radio and television programmes around the world.

Tom Arms is available to write articles, for lectures, or television and radio appearances for comment and analysis. His main areas of expertise are American and British politics and foreign policy, East-West relations, the Middle East and China.
